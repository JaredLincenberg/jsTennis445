About Game

Game Idea

In this game, You can play tennis against a computer. The ball will bounce oﬀ of your racket, the wall and the computer’s racket. With the goal to try to get it past the computer’s racket.

How To Play the Game

You can use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move the position of your player’s racket. You will lose the game if you miss 3 balls from the computer. Try to play more rounds to earn higher scores! Good luck!

Chanllege

With the goal to try to get it past the computer’s racket. As the number of times the ball gets past the computer increases, the computer’s racket speedup.

Score

The score will be recorded as the number of times the ball gets past the computer’s racket. The ball will move faster as the player scores more points. The high scores will be recorded for the best players.
