About Game

Game Idea

In this game, You can play tennis against a computer. The ball will bounce oﬀ of your racket and the computer’s racket. With the goal to try to earn as much points as you can.

How To Play the Game

You can use the 'W' key to move left and 'D' key to move right the position of your player’s racket. You will lose the game if you miss 3 balls from the computer. Try to play more rounds to earn higher scores! Good luck!

Chanllege

As the number of scores you earned increases, the computer’s racket speeds up.

Score

The score will be recorded as the number of round you play increases. The ball will move faster as the player scores more points. The high scores will be recorded for the best players.
